I am creating this website to showcase a few of the projects I have worked on during my time at Mizzou. I had a lot of fun creating these projects, so I hope you will find them interesting. One of the end goals for me is to have this website serve as a kind of hub where I can store information on all of my projects so I can share it with recruiters as a way of getting their attention and displaying my unique skills. As a bonus, it would be cool to have this place where I can look back on my projects myself and maybe share them with others as well.
